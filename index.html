Welcome to my academic website

I am Dr. Sarah Chen, an Associate Professor of Computer Science specializing in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. My research focuses on developing novel approaches to deep learning architectures and their applications in real-world scenarios.

Currently, I am leading the Intelligent Systems Laboratory at Stanford University, where I work with a talented group of graduate students and researchers on cutting-edge AI projects.

I have published 2 papers in NeurIPS 2024 and CVPR 2025.
